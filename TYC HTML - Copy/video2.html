VIDEO FEATURES

Just like the audio there are also features which make video play more awesome.

  • controls-this activates controls like play,pause,e.t.c.(< video controls>)
  • autoplay-this allows the video play when ready without the user clicking on play.(< video controls autoplay>)
  • autoplay loop- this makes the video replay when it ends.(< video controls autoplay loop>)
